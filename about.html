I'm a UX Designer, living in Redondo Beach in the Greater Los Angeles Area. I enjoy making life better by designing useful, intuitive, and delightful digital products. Performing thorough research, I am able to back up design decisions and create custom solutions for each project.

I graduated from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo with a Bachelor of Science degree in Food Science. In the food industry, I was a product developer, flavor chemist, and distiller. As the Research and Development Manager for a large snack foods company, I facilitated the development of dozens of products from ideation to launch.

Wanting more of an empathy-centric practice, I completed certificate programs in UX/UI Design and UX Research online from California Institute of the Arts, University of Michigan, and Georgia Tech. I completed the Full Stack Web Development bootcamp at UCLA Extension.

The crossroads of art and science allows me to research problems and try to best understand how to solve them and serve people, always keeping the emotional impact in mind. I enjoy creating designs that not only bring functional value to people, but also delight, making a positive emotional impact.

When I’m not designing digital products, I enjoy the outdoors, volleyball, pickleball, and learning about food and drink. I also enjoy photography, painting, music, and video games.
