Microgurt Website Design

Brief

Problem

Design the checkout process for an online e-commerce food delivery company.

Solution

Develop the UI for the checkout process of Microgurt, a custom made-to-order yogurt delivery company.

Description

E-Commerce food delivery company website

My Role

Solo Project: UX Design, UX Research, UI Design

Tools

Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator, Balsamiq, InVision

Platform

UI designs for desktop / laptop

Project Length

~1.5 months

When

2018

What is Microgurt?

Microgurt is a subscription-based, made-to-order yogurt delivery company that uses high quality ingredients with a focus on providing a substantial quantity of probiotic microorganisms to establish and maintain a healthy digestive system.

Why take on this project?

This project allowed me to work on and develop my e-commerce usability process.

Prototypes

Both the wireframe and high-fidelity prototypes can be viewed here, as well as further down this page, in context with the design process. These prototypes focus on the checkout process, so only relevant links are actionable. The wireframe prototype was created with Balsamiq. The high-fidelity prototype was created with Sketch and made interactive with InVision.

User and Client Needs

User Needs

Website needs to enable the user to:

Find out if the supplier delivers to their area

Order product online

Discern product description and custom ingredient ordering

Set up subscription orders and scheduling

Estimate prices

See order history

Contact someone with questions or issues about an order

Client Needs

Website needs to enable the client to:

Sell product online that will be delivered

Provide a system for order customization

Provide reliable communication and customer support

Communicate quality of the ingredients

Communicate health benefits of probiotics

Outline of Scope

Content Requirements

Food Menu
- display pricing
- show a list of pre-planned yogurt products as well as ingredients to choose from for custom yogurts

Images of product

Text indicating product is for South Bay area only

Contact section
- customer inquiries / support
- list company phone number and email address

About section - describe the company and the quality of the ingredients

Educational section - explain probiotics and best methods of consuming

Testimonials

FAQ section
- where do you deliver?
- what about dietary restrictions (allergens, dairy, etc.)?
- minimum order?
- additional charge for delivery?
- minimum time need for an order?
- pricing structure?
- contact?

Functionality Requirements

User enters delivery address and finds out if she is close enough to receive deliveries

Form to fill out and submit for orders; add items to shopping cart

Form to fill out for making changes / cancellation if necessary

Purchase as a guest or create a user account

Log in

In addition to accessing order history, logging in also allows user to set up a subscription for regular intervals of delivery (probiotics are best consumed daily for beneficial results) - time and date of delivery

Place order
- submit order form
- enter payment information
- get order confirmation (on website and receive email)

Mood Board

Mood boards are collages consisting of images, text, and samples of objects in a composition to portray the general feel and look of the website. Seen in the mood board below, the dark purples were used for buttons. As the website is selling a yogurt product, soft colors were utilized to give an almost “creamy” aesthetic. The foods here depict ingredients that may be added to the custom yogurt products. When ordering food online, it’s especially important to have photos of the food one is ordering.
