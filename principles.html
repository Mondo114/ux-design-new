Mayer’s Principles of Multimedia Learning:
A Video Lesson

VIEW VIDEO HERE

Brief

This educational video based on Mayer’s principles of multimedia learning was created as a project for the Master of Science in Instructional Design and Technology Program at Cal State University, Fullerton. It introduces the concept behind the principles, discusses a few of the principles as examples, and takes a deeper look at the Redundancy Principle and some related research studies.
