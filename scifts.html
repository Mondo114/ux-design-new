SCIFTS Website Redesign

Problem

The live SCIFTS website is outdated and has functionality, visual design, and information architecture issues.

Solution

Create new UI designs based on research that aim to improve the usability and visual aesthetics for the current SCIFTS website.

My Role

Solo Project: UX Research, UX Design, UI / Visual Design

Tools

Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator, InVision

Platform

Desktop / Laptop

Project Length

~6 weeks

What is SCIFTS?

The Institute of Food Technologists (IFT) is an international organization, founded in 1939 as a non-profit society. With over 23,000 members, the organization involves those working and studying food science, technology, nutrition, and similar topics in industry, academia and government.

The Southern California Institute of Food Technologists (SCIFTS) is one of the largest sections of IFT. SCIFTS puts on local networking events and talks of topical interest, funds student scholarships, and helps advance the science of food in Southern California.

Visit the current, live website at scifts.net

Why take on this project?

I was a member of SCIFTS for some time, having studied and practiced Food Science a number of years. I continue to have an interest in the food industry and have a desire to see SCIFTS offer the best informational resources it can.

Revitalizing the SCIFTS website allows me the opportunity to combine my past in Food Science with my current practice in UX Design.
