Spark6 Project "R"

Brief

Problem

The home-purchasing industry as a whole is missing a service provider to comprehensively connect potential buyers with real estate professionals.

Solution

The client wants to start a ratings and reviews website to help people make informed, educated decisions when choosing who to colaborate with for home-purchasing. The website will also allow professionals the opportunity to obtain feedback.

Description

Ratings and reviews website

My Role

Wireframes are completed,
starting work on visual design phase

Tools

Sketch, InVision, Photoshop

Platform

Desktop, tablet, phone (responsive)

Project Length

Iterative process over a few months

When

2020

Wireframes

Project "R" is codename for the project until an MVP has been launched. Wireframes have been completed, and visual design is in process.

To complete the wireframes, an initial round of requirements gathering was performed. I discussed the client’s goals and researched other platforms such as Angie’s List, Rate My Agent, and Reviews.io. I interviewed users of ratings and reviews platforms to determine what worked, desired features, and pain points. I presented a new iteration of interactive wireframes to the client at each meeting.

Once we determined the wireframes were at a point that communicated the features and functionality effectively, we were ready to move onto visual design. I will update with the visual design phase soon.

The screen below displays a view of the professional user’s experience. Industry professionals fill out the form about someone they collaborated with. A request is then sent to the recipient to provide feedback to the professional about the quality of work provided.
